First Attempts (Failure)

Before using AI to build a site, I explored multiple paths, seeking a way to generate income that fit me.

Questioning My Fit

At first, I believed that building a website might not suit my skills and preferences. Coding, design, and maintaining a full site felt overwhelming and slow, and I wasn’t confident this was the right approach.

Trying Multiple Methods

I didn’t give up easily. I experimented with blogging, creating YouTube content, and writing eBooks. Each method had potential, but without a clear system and consistent engagement, the results were minimal.

Lessons from Failure

These early attempts taught me that effort alone isn’t enough. I needed a structured system and the right tools to amplify my work. It was a frustrating, but essential step in discovering how AI could help me build a repeatable, productive process.
